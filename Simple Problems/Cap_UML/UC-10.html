Use Case Description

Use Case Task Assignment
Goal To assign specific operational tasks to team members.
Preconditions Team members exist (UC-09).
Success End Condition Task is assigned to a specific member with a deadline.
Failed End Condition Task remains unassigned.
Primary Actors Event Organizer / Team Lead
Secondary Actors Team Member (Assignee)
Trigger "Add Task" button.
Description / Main Success Scenario
StepAction
1Organizer opens "Kanban Board" or "Task List".
2Organizer creates new task (e.g., "Confirm Sound Check").
3Organizer assigns task to Team Member "Alex".
4Organizer sets due date and priority (High/Medium/Low).
5System saves task.
6System notifies "Alex" of new assignment.
Alternative Flows
StepBranching Action
3aReassignment: Organizer changes assignee from "Alex" to "Sam". System updates notifications.
Quality Requirements
StepRequirement
6Push notif. sent to mobile app (if applicable) within 5 seconds.
